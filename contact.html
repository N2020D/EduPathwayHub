Founder

EDUPathwayHub was founded by W.B.M.Niluka Dasuni, who is a strong advocate for higher education, especially for rural students. With a strong background in Information Technology, she aspires to provide a platform that bridges the gap between students and a wide range of educational opportunities available locally and globally, to students pursuing higher education.
As the founder of EduPathwayHub, I am committed to continually expanding and enhancing this platform to meet the evolving needs of students. Together, let’s make education more accessible, achievable, and impactful for everyone.

Founder’s personal journey

W.B.M.Niluka dasuni, Higher Education in Information Technology at SLIATE identified the difficulties students face in navigating the world of higher education. Her journey from identifying the right institutions to accessing reliable resources was marked by perseverance and determination that laid the foundation for EduPathwayHub.

To every student who visits EduPathwayHub, my message is simple: education is the most powerful tool you have to shape your future. This platform was built with you in mind—to guide you, support you, and help you unlock your true potential. The journey may not always be easy, but remember, every step brings you closer to your dreams.

Vision Behind EduPathwayHub

"The idea for EduPathwayHub stemmed from a desire to empower Sri Lankan students with the tools and information they need to make informed decisions about their educational paths. By bringing together resources such as government and foreign universities, scholarships, and career guidance into one centralized platform, Founder aims to simplify the process and inspire students to dream bigger.
