EXTERNAL DEGREE PROGRAMS

In Sri Lanka, external degree programs provide a flexible and accessible path for students to pursue higher education without attending full-time on-campus classes. These programs are designed to accommodate working individuals, students in rural areas, and those who want to balance education with other commitments. Our platform brings together information on recognized external degree programs offered by top Sri Lankan universities. Whether you're looking to enhance your qualifications in business, humanities, agriculture, or technology, this guide will help you explore the best opportunities available. Discover the right external degree program for you, access official university links, and take the next step in your academic journey!
