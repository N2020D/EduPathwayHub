Pivate Uversities and Istitutions

In Sri Lanka, several private universities and institutes offer external degree programs, providing flexible learning opportunities for students balancing education with other commitments.

Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT)

SLIIT is a leading non-state degree-awarding institute recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields such as Information Technology, Engineering, and Business Management.

Official Website

ESOFT Metro Campus

ESOFT Metro Campus provides various external degree programs, including the Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) in collaboration with the University of Moratuwa. It is recognized as an Accredited Course Provider by the British Computer Society (BCS) and offers Edexcel (UK) Higher National Diplomas (HNDs) in Computing and Business Management.

Official Website

Horizon Campus

Horizon Campus offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in disciplines such as Law, Information Technology, and International Relations. The campus is known for its modern facilities and commitment to providing quality education.

Official Website

Saegis Campus

Saegis Campus provides degree programs in fields like Business Management, Information Technology, and English. The campus emphasizes a student-centered learning environment and offers modern facilities to support academic pursuits.

Official Website

SAB Campus of CA Sri Lanka

The SAB Campus, affiliated with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CA Sri Lanka), offers programs in Accountancy and related fields. It aims to produce competent professionals equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge for the accounting profession.

Official Website

Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT)

Established in 1990, IIT offers specialized British degree programs in IT and Business through partnerships with international universities. The institute provides both undergraduate and postgraduate programs, catering to students seeking globally recognized qualifications.

Official Website

ICBT Campus

ICBT Campus offers a range of external degree programs in collaboration with international universities, providing flexible learning options in business and technology disciplines.

Official Website

Aquinas College of Higher Studies

Aquinas College offers external degree programs in various fields, including Arts, Business, and Science, providing opportunities for students to pursue higher education through flexible learning modes.

Official Website

National Institute of Business Management (NIBM)

NIBM offers external degree programs in collaboration with international universities, focusing on areas such as Business Management, Information Technology, and Languages, catering to students seeking flexible learning options.

Official Website

Open University of Sri Lanka (OUSL)

OUSL provides a wide range of external degree programs across various disciplines, utilizing open and distance learning methods to cater to a diverse student population seeking flexible higher education opportunities.

Official Website
